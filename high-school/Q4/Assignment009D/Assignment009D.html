Div Layouts - Floats

Divs are used to create sections of your page, including the header, left column, content, right column, and footer. We also sometimes throw in a few other divs to better organize the structure of a page. The trick to organizing the divs is the float property. When you float a div it floats the specified direction.

float:left; or float:right;

All following items (divs) flow next to the floated div.
